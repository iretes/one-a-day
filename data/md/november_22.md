<p>Namhansanseong was designed as an emergency capital for the Joseon dynasty (1392â€“1910), in a mountainous site 25Â km south-east of Seoul. Built and defended by Buddhist monk-soldiers, it could accommodate 4,000 people and fulfilled important administrative and military functions.
